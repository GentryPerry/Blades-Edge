## **Acknowledgements** 

_Blades ’68_ owes credit to many great games and game designers, including: 

- _a|state_ , by Morgan Davie, Jon Hodgson, and Handiwork Games. The Trouble Engine and backing factions come directly from this game, but it is also inspirational as a whole—tone, design, and presentation.
